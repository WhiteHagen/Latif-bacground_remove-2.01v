## 🌟 Overview
**Latif** is a specialized desktop utility designed for creators and photographers. Whether you need an instant crop of a green screen or a sophisticated AI-driven background extraction, Latif handles it with ease.

---

## ⚡ Two Core Engines

### 1. 🟢 Classic Chroma Key (Fast Mode)
* **Best for:** Green screens and blue screens.
* **Speed:** Instant.
* **Tech:** Traditional color-space thresholding.
* **Pros:** No AI overhead, perfect for low-end PCs.

### 2. 🧠 Smart AI Engine (Deep Learning)
* **Best for:** Complex backgrounds (streets, messy rooms, nature).
* **Speed:** Focused on precision.
* **Tech:** Powered by `rembg` and `onnxruntime`.
* **Pros:** High-quality edge detection (hair, fur, transparent objects).

---

## ⚙️ How It Works
* **Chroma Engine:** Uses math to subtract specific pixel values.
* **AI Engine:** Runs the **U2-Net** neural network. 
    > 💡 **Note:** On first use, the app will download the AI model (~170MB). Please ensure you have an active internet connection.
